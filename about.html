About Muni Educare

Extending Muni Model of Holistic Learning beyond its flagship school

Our Story

Muni Educare Coexistence Private Limited was established in the year 2017 to extend Muni Model of Holistic Learning, designed and developed at Muni International School's Research & Development Center, beyond its flagship school.

It is currently being implemented in a number of States in India in Government Schools and Private Schools. Muni Educare has also signed MOUs with education boards internationally in a number of countries including Japan and Cambodia.

The Muni Model represents a paradigm shift in educational methodology, focusing on holistic development, learner-centric approaches, and the integration of ancient wisdom with modern technology.
